About

This project was inspired by my own personal journey through journaling and self-reflection. After years of struggling to maintain a consistent journaling habit, I realized that the traditional pen-and-paper approach just wasn't working for me. I yearned for a more seamless, digital experience that could integrate with my busy lifestyle and adapt to my changing needs.

So, in 2023, I set out to create a digital journaling platform that would empower others to find their voice, explore their emotions, and cultivate greater self-awareness. This project is a portfolio piece for my time at Holberton School, where I've been honing my skills as a full-stack developer.
